Review for spanish food in Korea(spanish)

Spanish cuisine consists of the cooking traditions and practices from Spain. Olive oil (of which Spain is the world's largest producer) is heavily used in the Spanish cuisine,[1] and it is the base of many vegetable sauces (sofritos).[2] Hallmark herbs include parsley, oregano, rosemary and thyme.[3] The use of garlic has been noted as "common to all Spanish cooking".[4] Most common meats include chicken, pork, lamb and veal.[5] Fish and seafood are also consumed on a regular basis.[
